# Customer Churn Business Insights

## 1. Overall Churn Rate
26.58% of customers have churned, indicating that more than 1 in 4 customers leave the service.

## 2. Gender Has Minimal Impact
Male and female customers show almost identical churn rates, suggesting gender is not a significant predictor of churn.

## 3. Contract Type Strongly Influences Churn
Month-to-month customers have a churn rate above 42%, while two-year contract customers have a churn rate below 3%.

## 4. High Monthly Charges Increase Churn
Customers who churn pay higher average monthly charges than customers who stay.

## 5. Customer Tenure Reduces Churn
Customers who stay have an average tenure of approximately 38 months, compared to 18 months for churned customers.

## 6. Retention Focus Area
The highest-risk group consists of new customers on month-to-month contracts with higher monthly charges.
